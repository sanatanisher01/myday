Our standard cancellation policy offers:

  • Full refund if cancelled 30+ days before the event
  • 50% refund if cancelled 14-29 days before the event
  • No refund if cancelled less than 14 days before the event

Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for MyDay Events

Last Updated: March 31, 2025

At MyDay Events, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Booking details and preferences
  • Payment information
  • Communications with our team
  • Reviews and feedback
How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process bookings and manage your account
  • Communicate with you about bookings, services, and promotions
  • Improve our services and develop new offerings
  • Personalize your experience on our platform
  • Ensure the security of our website and services
Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers necessary to deliver our services
  • Partners with your explicit consent
  • Legal authorities when required by law
Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Opt out of marketing communications
Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on our website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@mydayevents.com.
